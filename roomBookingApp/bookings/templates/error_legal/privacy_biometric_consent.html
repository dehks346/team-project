RoomGuard Room Booking System

Your Biometric Data & Privacy

RoomGuard uses facial recognition to provide secure, contactless room access. We take your privacy extremely seriously. Below is a clear summary of what we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

📍 This system is powered by a Raspberry Pi + local face recognition model.
All biometric data stays on our secure server — nothing is sent to third-party cloud services.

1. What Biometric Data We Collect

  • Face embeddings (mathematical templates — not actual photos)
  • Multiple enrollment images (only during signup or re-enrollment)
  • Access attempt logs (timestamp, room, confidence score, result)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Real-time face verification when entering a room
  • Booking confirmation & access control
  • Security auditing (failed attempts, unauthorized access)
  • Improving the recognition model (only with explicit consent)

3. Data Retention & Security

Face templatesUntil you delete them or leave the organization
Access logs90 days (then auto-deleted)
Enrollment photosDeleted immediately after template creation

All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Only administrators can access logs.
